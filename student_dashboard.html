Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

Browse estates, click "View Details", then "Make Reservation". Fill out the form and wait for owner approval.

What happens after I submit a reservation?

The estate owner will review your request and either approve or reject it. You'll receive a notification with their decision.

Can I cancel a reservation?

Yes, you can cancel pending reservations. Contact the estate owner for confirmed reservations.
